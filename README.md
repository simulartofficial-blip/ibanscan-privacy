# Privacy Policy for IbanScan

**Last updated:** May 19, 2026

## Overview
IbanScan is a mobile application that scans images to detect IBAN numbers using AI technology.

## Data Collection
IbanScan does **not** collect, store, or share any personal data.

- Images you select are sent directly to Google Gemini API for IBAN detection and are not stored anywhere.
- Saved IBANs are stored **only on your device** using local storage.
- We do not collect names, email addresses, location data, or any other personal information.

## Third-Party Services
This app uses **Google Gemini API** to process images. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for their data handling practices.

## Children's Privacy
This app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
